# AI Apps Discovery

AI Apps Discovery helps teams identify high-potential AI products and turn ideas into build-ready concepts.

## What this page is for

- Discover AI app opportunities by market or user problem
- Compare app ideas by value, complexity, and speed to launch
- Prioritize MVP scope before development starts
- Align product, marketing, and technical teams on direction

## Discovery framework

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define target users">
    Start with one clear audience and one high-value problem.
  </Step>
  <Step title="List AI-enabled solutions">
    Brainstorm 5-10 possible app concepts that use AI to improve outcomes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Score each concept">
    Rank ideas by user demand, implementation effort, and revenue potential.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select one MVP">
    Choose the smallest version that proves value quickly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example AI app categories

- AI assistants for operations teams
- AI copilots for sales and support
- AI tools for content and marketing workflows
- AI apps for data summarization and reporting
- AI-powered mobile productivity apps
