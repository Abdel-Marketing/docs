# AI Apps Discovery

Use this area to find strong AI product opportunities before you start building.

## What it helps you do
- Browse curated AI app ideas by category and use case
- Inspect opportunity details before committing build time
- Compare ideas based on audience, problem, and launch potential
- Turn promising ideas directly into LaunchPulse projects

## Typical workflow
1. Explore ideas aligned to your market or audience
2. Shortlist opportunities with clear demand and simple first versions
3. Pick one idea and define the core user + main problem
4. Convert it into a LaunchPulse build and start iterating

## Best for
- Founders choosing what to build next
- Creators testing multiple app concepts
- Small teams validating opportunities quickly
