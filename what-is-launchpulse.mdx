# What is LaunchPulse?

LaunchPulse is a product-building workspace for founders and small teams who want to ship real apps faster. You describe the outcome you want, shape the plan before build starts, and then iterate until your core user flow is ready to launch.

## Start here

<Steps>
  <Step title="Understand the core flow">

  </Step>
  <Step title="Ship your first project">
    Follow the Quickstart to create and validate your first MVP.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your build path">
    Start with Autonomous mode, then add Feature Builder or Custom Agents as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you can build

- SaaS MVPs with login and paid plans
- Internal tools for operations or reporting
- Mobile app MVPs with store publishing workflows
- AI-enabled products such as assistants, summaries, and smart search

## How LaunchPulse works

| Stage | What happens | Your role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Prompt | You describe your product idea | Provide clear context and goals |
| Plan | LaunchPulse generates a feature plan/backlog | Remove, reorder, and refine scope |
| Build | AI build workflow implements your app | Monitor progress and quality |
| Iterate | You request focused improvements | Prioritise one change at a time |
| Launch | Deploy web or publish mobile | Validate and release confidently |

## Example first prompt

```text
Build a web app for independent recruiters.

Core problem:
They need a simple way to track candidates and client roles.

Must-have features:
1) Candidate pipeline board
2) Client/company records
3) Role-based login (owner + recruiter)

Success for v1:
- Recruiters can move candidates through stages
- Owner can view team activity by role
```
