# What is LaunchPulse?

LaunchPulse is a workspace that helps you go from idea to a working app.

## When to use it

- You want to ship an MVP quickly
- You want one place for build, iteration, and launch workflows
- You prefer plain-English prompts over tool-by-tool setup

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe your idea">
    Start a project and explain what you want to build.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the plan">
    LaunchPulse generates a plan and feature list. You can edit priorities before build starts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build, test, and iterate">
    LaunchPulse builds your app, then you request focused improvements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch">
    Publish your app when your core user flow is ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>
