# What is LaunchPulse?

LaunchPulse is an AI-powered software creation platform that helps you turn ideas into real products by describing what you want.

You can use LaunchPulse to build web apps, mobile apps, dashboards, games, workflows, internal tools, SaaS MVPs, and other custom software—without a heavy technical setup.

<Info>
LaunchPulse is designed for speed: start from a prompt, refine with AI, and move toward a launch-ready product.
</Info>

## How LaunchPulse works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with an idea">
    Begin with your own concept or choose one from the AI app ideas catalog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe what you want to build">
    Explain your users, the problem, and the outcomes you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and refine the plan">
    LaunchPulse creates a plan you can edit before building.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview the product live">
    Move from plan to a working product preview you can validate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Iterate with prompts">
    Request improvements, feature changes, and refinements in plain language.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy or publish">
    Deploy web apps, connect domains, or publish mobile apps to app stores.
  </Step>
</Steps>
