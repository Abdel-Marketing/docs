# Feature Builder

Feature Builder helps you avoid vague builds by turning your concept into a practical, launch-ready feature plan.

## When to use this
- Your idea is broad and needs focus
- You want MVP-first scope discipline

## How it works
- Parses your prompt into feature groups
- Suggests priorities and sequencing
- Lets you edit before execution

## What you can do with it
- Cut low-value scope early
- Protect time-to-launch
- Align team on what v1 means

## Example use cases
- Trim a 20-feature concept into a 5-feature MVP
- Move revenue-critical features to top priority
