# Custom Domain

## Overview
Connect your own domain to your deployed LaunchPulse web app.

## Before you start
- A deployed web app
- Access to your domain registrar DNS settings

## Step-by-step guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="1) Open Custom Domain settings">
    In your project, open the Custom Domain area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="2) Enter your domain">
    Add the domain or subdomain you want to use.
  </Step>
  <Step title="3) Add DNS records">
    Copy the records LaunchPulse provides and add them at your registrar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="4) Wait for DNS + SSL">
    After propagation, LaunchPulse provisions SSL and your domain goes live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next
- Test your live URL
- Confirm redirects and SSL behavior
