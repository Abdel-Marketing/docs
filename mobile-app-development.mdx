# Mobile App Development

LaunchPulse helps you turn a mobile app idea into a working mobile-first product. Describe what you want to build, preview the app as it takes shape, test it on a real device, and prepare it for publishing to iOS and Android.

Use Mobile App Development when your product needs to feel native, portable, and easy to use on the go.

<Info>
Mobile apps are best when your users need quick access from their phone, push-style workflows, camera/location-style experiences, or an app-store-ready product.
</Info>

## When to choose a mobile app

Choose **Mobile App** when:

- your users will mostly interact from their phone
- you want an app-store-ready product
- the experience should feel focused and native
- you need a mobile-first MVP for testing
- you want to preview and test the app on a real device
- you plan to publish to iOS, Android, or both

Choose **Web App** instead if your product is mainly a dashboard, admin panel, SaaS workspace, or tool that users will access from desktop.

## How mobile app building works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a new mobile project">
    Create a new project and choose **Mobile App** as the app type. Describe the product you want to build, who it is for, and the main features it needs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Let LaunchPulse generate the first version">
    LaunchPulse creates the first mobile app structure, screens, flows, and interface based on your prompt.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Preview the app">
    Use the built-in preview to check the mobile experience while the app is being built.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test on a real device">
    Use the mobile preview QR code to open the app on your phone and test the experience more realistically.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Iterate with prompts">
    Ask LaunchPulse to improve screens, add flows, fix issues, refine design, or adjust the user journey.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish when ready">
    Prepare your app for iOS and Android publishing once the core experience is working.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Mobile app workflow

| Stage | What happens | What you should check |
|---|---|---|
| Idea | You describe the app, users, and core problem | Is the app clearly mobile-first? |
| First build | LaunchPulse generates the initial app | Are the main screens and flows present? |
| Preview | You test the app in LaunchPulse | Does the app look good on mobile dimensions? |
| Device test | You scan the QR code and open the app on your phone | Does the experience feel natural on a real device? |
| Iterate | You request improvements through prompts | Are you improving one focused flow at a time? |
| Publish | You prepare for iOS and Android release | Are store assets, app details, and core flows ready? |
