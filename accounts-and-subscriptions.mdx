# Accounts & Subscriptions

LaunchPulse plans are built around one simple idea: start building for free, then upgrade when you are ready to ship, deploy, publish, collaborate, or scale.

Each plan gives you a monthly AI credit allowance. Credits power the AI workflows inside LaunchPulse, including app building, app updates, screenshot generation, and AI app discovery.

<Info>
  Free is for exploring. Starter is for shipping. Builder is for serious builders who need more credits, stronger testing, and faster iteration.
</Info>

## Free

Free is designed for trying LaunchPulse before committing to a paid plan.

Use Free when you want to:

- test the LaunchPulse workflow
- create small experiments
- preview an app idea
- try the AI App Store screenshot generator
- understand how credits work
- explore before upgrading

Free includes:

- **\$0/month**
- **10,000 AI credits/month**
- live preview
- AI App Store screenshot generator

Free is useful for exploration, but it is not designed for launching production apps.

## Starter

Starter is the main plan for builders who are ready to ship.

Use Starter when you want to:

- build real apps
- create unlimited projects
- deploy web apps
- connect custom domains
- publish mobile apps
- download generated code
- collaborate in a workspace
- generate and download app store screenshots

Starter includes:

- **\$19/month**
- **200,000 AI credits/month**
- unlimited projects
- live preview and testing
- LaunchPulse Cloud
- publish to iOS App Store and Android Play Store
- one-click deployment for websites and web apps
- custom domains
- workspace / collaboration
- download code
- unlimited app store screenshot downloads

<Tip>
  Starter is the best plan for most users who want to move from experimenting to actually launching.
</Tip>

## Builder

Builder is for users who are building more often, testing more deeply, or running larger workflows.

Use Builder when you want:

- more monthly AI credits
- faster iteration
- more advanced browser testing
- autobuild
- priority support
- stronger collaboration and project sharing
- more room for bigger projects and repeated build sessions

Builder includes:

- **\$49/month**
- everything in Starter
- **500,000 AI credits/month**
- autobuild
- advanced browser testing
- priority support
- stronger project sharing and collaboration workflows

## Monthly vs annual billing

LaunchPulse may offer annual billing with savings compared to monthly billing.

| Plan | Monthly | Annual equivalent |
| --- | --: | --: |
| Starter | \$19/month | about \$15/month when billed annually |
| Builder | \$49/month | about \$39/month when billed annually |

Annual billing is best when LaunchPulse is already part of your normal building workflow.

## Credit add-ons

If you run out of credits before your monthly reset, you can buy extra credit packs.

Credit add-ons are useful when:

- you are close to finishing a project
- you need more build iterations
- you are generating many app screenshots
- you are using AI app discovery heavily
- you want more credits without changing your plan immediately

| Add-on | Price |
| --- | --: |
| 50K credits | \$5 |
| 100K credits | \$9 |
| 500K credits | \$39 |
| 1M credits | \$69 |

<Info>
  Credit add-ons are one-time purchases. They help you keep building when your monthly credits run low.
</Info>

## What happens when you run out of credits

When you run out of credits, AI-powered actions may be limited until you:

- wait for your monthly credits to reset
- purchase a credit add-on
- upgrade to a higher plan

If you often run out of credits, Builder may be a better fit than repeatedly buying smaller add-ons.

## Common questions

### Can I deploy on the Free plan?

No. Deployment requires a paid subscription.

### Can I download code on the Free plan?

No. Code download requires a paid subscription.

### Can I connect a custom domain on the Free plan?

No. Custom domains require a paid subscription.

### Do screenshots use credits?

Yes. App Store and Play Store screenshot generation uses credits.

### Does AI app discovery use credits?

Yes. AI app discovery and mobile AI research workflows are credit-based.

### What happens if I run out of credits?

You can wait for your monthly reset, buy add-on credits, or upgrade to a plan with more monthly credits.

### Which paid plan should most users start with?

Most users should start with Starter because it unlocks the core shipping workflow: unlimited projects, deployment, custom domains, mobile publishing, collaboration, and code download.

## Account and billing checklist

Before upgrading, check:

- how many projects you plan to build
- whether you need deployment
- whether you need a custom domain
- whether you need to download code
- whether you are publishing to App Store or Play Store
- how often you use screenshot generation
- how often you use AI app discovery or mobile research
- whether advanced browser testing matters for your workflow
- whether you need priority support
