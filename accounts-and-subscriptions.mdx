# Accounts & Subscriptions

This page helps you plan spend and avoid build interruptions by understanding how subscriptions and credits work.

## When to use this
- Choosing between plans
- Tracking credit burn during active builds
- Understanding usage-based costs

## How it works
- Monthly plan includes credits
- Top-ups extend build capacity
- Storage/database usage billed separately
