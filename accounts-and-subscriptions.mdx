# Accounts & Subscriptions

## Overview
LaunchPulse uses subscription plans with credits for build work, plus usage-based charges for storage and database.

## When to use it
- Choose the right monthly plan
- Track credits during active building
- Understand separate usage charges

## Plan snapshot (from source docs)

| Plan | Price | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Starter | $20/month | Includes monthly credit balance |
| Builder | $50/month | Includes larger monthly credit balance |

## Billing concepts

| Concept | What it covers |
| --- | --- |
| Credits | Building, feature work, iterations, AI usage, deployment workflows |
| Top-ups | Extra credits when you run out mid-cycle |
| Usage-based charges | Storage and database usage |

## How it works
- Your plan gives monthly credits
- You can buy top-ups as needed
- Storage/database usage is billed separately
