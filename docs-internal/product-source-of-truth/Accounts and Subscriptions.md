## Overview

LaunchPulse V2 introduces **Credits** to make it easier to build, iterate, and keep moving when you get stuck. Instead of worrying about separate tools and complicated billing, your LaunchPulse account bundles the core build experience into a simple subscription + credits model — and handles infrastructure usage (like storage and databases) in a clear, separate way.

---

## Monthly plans

LaunchPulse V2 currently offers two monthly subscriptions:

| Plan | Price | What you get |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Starter | **$20/month** | Monthly credit balance for builds and iteration |
| Builder | **$50/month** | Larger monthly credit balance, more headroom for bigger projects |

Each plan includes a monthly credit balance to power building, iteration, and AI-assisted development.

---

## Add-on credits (top-ups)

If you run out of credits mid-build, you can purchase **credit add-ons** so you can keep shipping without waiting for the next billing cycle.

Useful when you're:
- iterating quickly
- testing multiple ideas
- building a bigger MVP in a short time

---

## What your credits cover

Credits are designed to cover the core "building work" inside LaunchPulse:

| Area | What credits pay for |
| --- | --- |
| **Project build** | The agent's work generating and implementing your app across the stack |
| **Feature creation & suggestions** | When LaunchPulse proposes feature sets, improvements, or planning outputs and then builds them |
| **Iterations** | Updating, refining, and reworking your app as you go |
| **AI credits** | Building AI functionality and using AI capabilities inside your project workflow |
| **Deployment** | Publishing and deploying your project, including release workflows |

---

## Pay-as-you-go usage (storage + database)

In addition to credits, LaunchPulse supports **pay-as-you-go** charges for usage-based infrastructure — so you only pay for what you actually consume.

This applies to:
- **Storage** (files, uploads, assets)
- **Database usage** (data stored, reads/writes depending on the underlying provider)

This keeps costs fair and scalable: small projects stay light, and bigger projects can grow without forcing everyone into the same flat fee.

---

## Managing your subscription

From your account area you can:
- view your current plan and billing status
- monitor your credit balance (and top up with add-ons)
- upgrade or downgrade between the $20 and $50 plans as your needs change
