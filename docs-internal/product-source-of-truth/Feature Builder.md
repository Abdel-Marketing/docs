## Overview

LaunchPulse V2 includes a rethought **Feature Builder** designed to help users create higher-quality apps. Instead of starting from a blank page (or random outputs), Feature Builder turns your idea into a structured product plan, suggests beneficial features, and helps you build in the right order — fast.

---

## What it does

### 1) Turns your idea into a clear plan
When you describe what you want, LaunchPulse generates:
- a **build plan**
- a **feature list** your app actually needs (not just what you asked for, but what makes it complete)
- a logical **priority order** so the product can launch as an MVP and improve over time

### 2) Suggests improvements and beneficial features
The system recommends features that improve the app's chances of success:
- onboarding and user flows
- core MVP features vs nice-to-haves
- retention / engagement improvements
- monetisation readiness
- UX and "polish" features that make the app feel complete

These are **suggestions, not forced additions** — you stay in control.

---

## You keep full control

Before anything is built, you can:
- **Remove features** you don't want yet
- **Reorder priorities** (ship MVP first, or focus on a specific feature)
- **Inject new context** (target users, constraints, examples, tone, must-have requirements)

This makes the AI predictable and aligned with your goals.

---

## Build after approval

Once you approve the plan:
- LaunchPulse builds the app **end-to-end**
- the build runs **on autopilot**
- you can track progress as the app comes to life

---

## Quick recap

- Generates a plan + a feature list your app actually needs
- Suggests improvements to increase usefulness and quality
- You control what gets built and in what order
- After approval, LaunchPulse builds end-to-end on autopilot
