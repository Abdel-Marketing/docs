## Overview

Every app built with **LaunchPulse** comes with **native access to AI capabilities** through built-in LaunchPulse AI services. You can build AI-powered products without setting up provider accounts, managing API keys, or worrying about the underlying infrastructure.

---

## Why this matters

Building AI features usually means dealing with:
- API keys and secret storage
- provider setup and billing
- infrastructure and environment configuration
- integration work across backend + frontend

LaunchPulse keeps AI under one roof, so you can focus on the product instead of the plumbing.

---

## How it works

### 1) Ask the agent to add AI to your app
You describe what you want in plain English, and the agent connects the AI services for you.

Example prompts:
- "Add an AI chat assistant to my app."
- "Summarise user documents and show key takeaways."
- "Generate product descriptions from a title and bullets."
- "Add AI search across my content."

### 2) No API keys required
You don't create, copy, or maintain keys. LaunchPulse provides access to AI services directly inside the platform — your project can use AI features without you managing credentials.

### 3) Everything runs under one hood
LaunchPulse handles:
- connecting AI services to your app
- implementing the UI and backend logic needed to use AI features
- ensuring the integration works as part of the overall build workflow

---

## What you can build

- Chat assistants and copilots
- Content generation (text, descriptions, emails, FAQs)
- Summarisation and extraction
- Q&A over your app's content
- Smart onboarding and form help
- AI-powered search and recommendations

---

## Credits for AI Services

AI usage inside your built apps is covered through LaunchPulse credits — the same balance that powers builds and iterations. This means there's no separate AI bill to manage on top of your subscription.

For high-volume AI apps, top-up credit add-ons let you keep running without waiting for the next billing cycle. See the Accounts & Subscriptions page.
