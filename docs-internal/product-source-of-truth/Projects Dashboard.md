**Status:** ✅ Live  
**Area:** Onboarding  
**Owner:** _TBD_

---

## Overview

The **Projects** area is where every app you build with LaunchPulse lives. It's the home base for switching between projects, picking up where you left off, and reviewing past work.

---

## Where to find it

You can find all your projects in the **sidebar** — look for the file icon. Click it to open the projects view.

---

## What you can do from here

- **Open** any existing project and resume building
- **Create** a new project (Mobile App or Web App)
- **Switch** between active projects without losing context
- **Review** project status (in build, deployed, archived)
- **Share** projects with collaborators (see Collaboration page)
