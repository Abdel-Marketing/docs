# Screenshots Mobile App

Use this page to document how your team captures and manages screenshots during mobile app development.

## Why screenshots matter

- Track UI progress over time
- Share visual bugs quickly with your team
- Prepare App Store and Play Store assets
- Validate responsive behavior across devices

## Suggested screenshot workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Capture key screens">
    Take screenshots of onboarding, authentication, home, feature flows, and error states.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Label each image clearly">
    Include screen name, platform (iOS/Android), and version.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Store in one place">
    Keep screenshots grouped by release so product, design, and QA can review quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review before publishing">
    Validate branding, copy, and feature accuracy before submitting to app stores.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

- Keep screenshots consistent in language and branding
- Avoid placeholder data in store-facing images
- Re-capture screenshots after major UI updates
- Use high-quality images with readable text
