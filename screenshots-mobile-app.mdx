# Screenshots Mobile App

Use this workspace when you need store-ready mobile screenshots that look clean, consistent, and conversion-focused.

## What it helps you do
- Generate App Store / Play Store screenshot sets
- Paste an App Store or Play Store app link to start from an existing app
- Upload your own app screens and design references
- Refine style, copy, and visual hierarchy for stronger listings

## Typical workflow
1. Choose your platform and target screenshot sizes
2. Add an app store link or upload real app screens
3. Provide references for branding, tone, and layout style
4. Generate screenshot concepts and iterate with prompts
5. Export polished assets for submission

## Best for
- Teams preparing a new app launch
- Founders improving store listing conversion
- Agencies producing app store creative quickly
